# Personas

## Startup Founder or Founding Engineer

Needs:

- fast path from repository to production
- infrastructure and rollout defaults that do not require a platform team
- confidence that early production changes are safe

Success for this persona means faster shipping with fewer self-inflicted outages.

## Platform or DevOps Lead

Needs:

- consistent control across services and environments
- safer release governance without brittle internal tooling
- visibility into ownership, policies, and rollout health

Success means fewer bespoke scripts, better standardization, and clear operational posture.

## Engineering Manager or CTO

Needs:

- confidence that critical changes are reviewed and explainable
- visibility into operational risk and change throughput
- a platform that scales with the company instead of being replaced every year

Success means trust, velocity, and governance improving together.

## Enterprise Security or Compliance Leader

Needs:

- auditability of privileged and production-impacting actions
- policy enforcement hooks
- evidence collection and traceability
- progressive adoption across existing tools

Success means stronger control without freezing delivery.
